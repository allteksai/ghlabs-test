WELCOME TO GLOBAL HARVEST Labs

Global Harvest Labs is a research and development lab with scientific sophistication and experience. We offer a surplus of pharmaceutical drug development services including; preclinical or clinical dosage formulation development, analytical methods development and testing, stability storage and testing, and in-house and collaborative full product formulation development.

Services At A Glance

Global Harvest Labs offers a full range of services provided by skilled professionals using the qualified pharmaceutical instrument and equipment. The Laboratories are managed following GLP/GMP guidelines. Our services include:

Analytical Testing
  • SPECTROSCOPY: FTIR Spectroscopy, UV-Vis spectroscopy
  • CHROMATOGRAPHY: GC, HPLC
  • BIOAVAILABILITY: Partition coefficient, Log D
  • Dissolution (USP I, II) ,
  • General Chemistry, pKa, pH and solubility
     
Analytical Method Development
  • Biorelavent dissolution methods (FDA)
  • HPLC method development and validation (FDA)
  • GC method development and Validation (FDA)
  • Protocol for stability testing (ICH)
  • Cleaning validation methods and validation (FDA)
     
Formulation Development (FINISHED PRODUCT AND PRECLINICAL OR CLINICAL DOSAGE FORMS)
  • Small Volume Injections:

  • Oral solids:

    • Tablet profits testing

  • Oral liquid
    • Syrups
    • Suspension
    • Pouches
  • Topical
